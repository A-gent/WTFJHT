About

WTF Just Happened, Today is a personal project to log the daily shock and awe in Trump's America. The goal is to capture the most important news in a digestable form inspired by The Skimm and tweetstorms.

I started this project because I was having trouble keeping up with the pace of news coming out from the Trump administration. It started as an Instapaper and Evernote notebook, but quickly became unwieldy as news events started to blur. So, I started logging the things on a day-by-day basis.

This is, strictly speaking, the news through my lens and not a professional act of journalism, although, it is an act of journalism in and of itself. You won't find "fair and balanced" objective coverage. What you will find, however, is an independent voice, personal accountability, and a consideration for how the editorial choices I make impact others.

This project is hosted on GitHub Pages and the public GitHub repository can be found here.
